Key Features

Powerful capabilities built on blockchain technology

Blockchain-Secured Records

All vehicle records are stored on an immutable blockchain, ensuring data integrity and preventing unauthorized modifications.

Vehicle Registration & Ownership Transfer

Streamlined registration process and secure ownership transfers with complete transaction history and verification.

Insurance & HPG Verification

Integrated verification system for insurance providers and Highway Patrol Group clearance with real-time status updates.

OR/CR Digital Certificates

Digital Official Receipt and Certificate of Registration with QR codes for instant verification and easy access.

Fast & Tamper-Proof Transactions

Lightning-fast processing with complete transparency. Every transaction is recorded on the blockchain for audit purposes.

Multi-Role Access Control

Secure access for vehicle owners, LTO administrators, insurance verifiers, emission centers, and HPG personnel.

How It Works

Simple, secure, and transparent process

1

User Registers Vehicle

Vehicle owner submits registration documents through the secure portal with all required information.

2

LTO Admin Verifies

LTO administrators review and verify all submitted documents for accuracy and compliance.

3

HPG & Insurance Approval

Highway Patrol Group and Insurance providers verify clearance and coverage requirements.

4

Blockchain Record Creation

Verified data is permanently recorded on the LTO Network blockchain, creating an immutable record.

5

Digital OR/CR Issued

Digital Official Receipt and Certificate of Registration are generated and made available to the owner.
